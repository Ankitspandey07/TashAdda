LONG DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TashAdda is a private card table for you and your friends — not a casino with random opponents.

Host a room, share a code, or sit on the same Wi‑Fi. Only people you invite join your table. There is no public lobby and no matchmaking with strangers.

HOW YOU PLAY

• Play vs Bots — practice on one phone with 2–4 bots at your chosen boot and chip limit.

• Play Online — create a room, get a 6-letter code, share it on WhatsApp or SMS, and friends join from anywhere.

• Local Room — same Wi‑Fi or mobile hotspot; the host broadcasts the table and friends tap Join.

• Bluff (Cheat) — call bluffs, play cards face-down, and win when opponents catch you or you empty your hand.

GAMES

Classic 3-card, Muflis (lowball), and Bluff are fully playable. More variants coming soon.

TASHADDA TABLE RULES

Fair house rules built in: starting chips reset each round, boot ante, blind and seen chaals, sideshow, show or pack when you are short on chips, and a session scoreboard when you leave.

FEATURES

• Private rooms only — friends you invite
• Chat and emoji at the table
• Profile photo on your seat
• Session won/lost summary
• Clear in-app rules sheet
• Works on phone and tablet layouts

TashAdda is meant for friendly home games with people you know. Download, invite your group, and deal.
