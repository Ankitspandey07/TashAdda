LONG DESCRIPTION — FULL FEATURE LIST
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TashAdda is a private multiplayer card table built for playing with people you know — friends, family, and your usual home-game group. It is not a casino app and there is no public matchmaking. You never sit with random strangers. You host a room, share a code or join on the same Wi‑Fi, and only the people you invite play at your table.

WHY TASHADDA IS DIFFERENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Unlike most card apps that drop you into lobbies with unknown players, TashAdda works like a real home game:

  • Private rooms only — you control who joins
  • No public matchmaking or stranger tables
  • Host sets boot (ante) and chip limit for everyone in the room
  • Play on one phone, over the internet, or on the same Wi‑Fi/hotspot
  • Optional bots fill empty seats when you need a third player

THREE WAYS TO PLAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

▸ PLAY VS BOTS (solo on one device)
  Practice or play alone on a single phone. Pick your game, set starting chips
  and boot, choose 2–4 bots, and deal. Perfect for learning rules or when
  no one else is free.

▸ PLAY ONLINE (anywhere — room code)
  Host creates a room and gets a 6-letter code. Share it on WhatsApp, SMS, or
  any chat app. Friends enter the code and join from anywhere. No IP addresses,
  no complicated setup — just your private table on the internet.

▸ LOCAL ROOM (same Wi‑Fi or mobile hotspot)
  Host broadcasts a table on the local network. Friends open Search Local Rooms
  and tap Join. Great for house parties, travel, or when everyone is in the
  same room on one hotspot.

GAMES INCLUDED
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

▸ CLASSIC (3-card)
  The familiar Indian 3-card game. Trail (three of a kind) beats everything.
  Runs blind and seen chaals, sideshow, show, and full TashAdda house rules.

▸ MUFLIS (lowball)
  Same table and betting flow, but the weakest hand wins. A fun twist when
  your group wants something different.

▸ BLUFF (CHEAT)
  A separate fast-paced game: play cards face-down, claim ranks, call bluffs,
  and empty your hand to win. Supports the same online and LAN multiplayer
  as the card-table games.

▸ COMING SOON
  Poker, Rummy, and more variants are on the roadmap.

TASHADDA TABLE RULES (BUILT IN)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Fair, consistent house rules so every round plays the same:

  • Starting chips reset each round (host picks limit, e.g. 200 or 1000)
  • Boot (ante) posted by every player at the start of each round
  • Blind phase: up to 8 chaals while cards may stay hidden
  • Seen players pay double during the blind phase
  • After 8 chaals, all active players see their cards automatically
  • Equal betting after auto-show — no more seen double
  • Maximum 25 chaals per round, then forced showdown
  • Sideshow: challenge a seen opponent — they accept or reject (5 second
    timeout) — weaker hand packs
  • Show: when two players remain, pay and compare hands — winner takes pot
  • Broke? If you cannot afford the next chaal, Show or Pack
  • Zero chips = packed; last player standing wins

Full rules are always one tap away in the app (TashAdda rules sheet).
